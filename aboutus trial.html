About Us

Welcome to Cafe Amistoso, a cozy space where great conversations happen over delicious food and beverages. Founded by Hiral Panchal in the heart of Vadodara, our café is dedicated to providing a warm and inviting atmosphere for everyone.

At Cafe Amistoso, we believe in quality and freshness. Our menu features a variety of artisanal coffees, handcrafted pastries, and light meals, all made with love and the finest ingredients.

Join us for a cup of coffee, a delightful treat, or simply to unwind with friends. We look forward to welcoming you to our café!
